Notes

  1. Intro to cheese fries

    • Always make sure you season your french fry in any way
    • seasoning can include ketchup, soysauce, salt, pepper, cheese, etc.
    • Fries can be crispy, soft, potatoey, big, small, and much more!

  2. Cheeses

    • there are over 1500 types of cheese from mozzerella to swiss. Most I think are good for fries.
    • To have the best cheese fry experience, put the cheese on a warm fry/fries OR melt the cheese then pour it on the fry/fries.
